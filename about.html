About Us

Global One Kenya is an affiliate of GO UK which is an international NGO that was founded on the belief that there is a need and possibility for more women to come forward as change-makers in positions of leadership because when women everywhere are empowered they transform their lives, communities and the world.

Our Vision

A world where women of all beliefs are empowered to make their own choices and transform their communities, inspired by an understanding of faith and cultural perspectives.

Our Mission

To develop innovative sustainable solutions that is faith inspired and improves the livelihoods and wellbeing of all women.

Our Strategy




Our strategy is to empower and work with community gatekeepers; build an influential community, women and religious leaders,
to provide sustainable support to women by tapping in to their strongly-held beliefs.
